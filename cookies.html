Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Setuvio uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and improve our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality like security and accessibility.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website, allowing us to improve performance and user experience.

Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences such as language and theme settings.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies. These services include analytics providers and embedded content.

Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

For questions about our use of cookies, contact us at privacy@setuvio.com.
